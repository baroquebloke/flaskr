What Brought Me Here?
...

When I was younger I fell in love with music. This proceeded to guide my life for well over a decade, through highschool, a couple years at Florida State University, and finally to Oberlin Conservatory. While there I met a girl who, unbeknownst to me, would steal me away to France and eventually become my wife.

Though we're stateside again those two years abroad helped me realize that, despite my love for music, performance did not allow me to use my mind in the ways I wanted. So with the help of my wife, cheap wine, and expensive moldy cheese I made the decision to head in a different direction: programming.

In a way programming is not unlike music and French combined. There is still the challenge and excitement of learning a foreign language but also the thrill of creating something beautiful. I've been hooked ever since.
